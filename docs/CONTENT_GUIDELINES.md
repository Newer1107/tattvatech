# Content Guidelines

## Approved Vertical Names

- TattvaTech Services
- TattvaTech Products
- TattvaTech Drones
- TattvaTech Training

## Approved Service Names

- Web Development
- Software Development
- Mobile App Development
- AI & Automation
- Cloud & DevOps
- Data Analytics
- UI/UX Design

## Tone

- Clear
- Technical
- Warm
- Confident
- Disciplined
- Forward-looking without exaggeration

## Factual Content Rules

- Do not fabricate employee count, revenue, office locations, awards, client logos, or years of operation.
- Do not imply products are launched unless approved launch content exists.
- Do not overstate the maturity of drones or training verticals.
- Keep founder-led and early-stage positioning honest.

## Testimonials and Achievements

- Never publish fake testimonials.
- Never publish fake achievements.
- Placeholder sections may explain that approved public proof is still pending.
- Any future social proof must be attributable and explicitly approved.
